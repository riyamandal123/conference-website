ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

8th International Conference on “Sustainable Materials And Recent Trends in Mechanical Engineering” (SMARTME-2024)” will be held at REVA University, Bengaluru, India.The conference will have plenary lectures followed by parallel sessions. Plenary lectures will be delivered by eminent personalities of repute to introduce the theme of the conference. Conference is to provide a platform to PG students, Researchers, Young aspiring Engineers & Academicians to present their research findings of their respective domains of knowledge and share ideas and views.
